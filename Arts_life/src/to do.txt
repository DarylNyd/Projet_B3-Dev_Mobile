To run app on your phone without using expo:
1. run app on emulator
2.press ctrl + m
3.go to dev settings or settings
4. go to debug server host & port for device
5. type in your ip address and port number which is 10.0.2.2:80
6. press ok
7.close emulator
8.connect your phone to your computer
9. run app on your phone
10.authorize your phone to run or/and install app
